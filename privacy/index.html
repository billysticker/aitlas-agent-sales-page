Privacy & Cookies

This page explains how ChiroCandy uses cookies and similar technologies on aitlasagent.com for visitors worldwide.

Who we are

Aitlas Agent is a product of ChiroCandy. This site is operated by ChiroCandy. For privacy questions, contact us at the email published on chirocandy.com or through your ChiroCandy account manager.

What we collect on this site

  • Essential site data — pages you load, security signals, and preferences that make the site work (including your cookie choice).
  • Forms & checkout — information you submit when registering for training, booking a call, or completing a purchase (handled by our form and payment providers).
  • Optional analytics — only if you allow analytics cookies: aggregated traffic and engagement data to improve the site.
  • Optional marketing — only if you allow marketing cookies: ad measurement and retargeting for campaigns we run.

Cookie categories

Essential — always on. Needed for core site function, security, and storing your consent decision. Includes local storage used for consent and, on the limited-offer page, the founding countdown timer.

Analytics — optional. Helps us understand how the site is used. Loaded only after you accept analytics.

Marketing — optional. Used for advertising measurement where applicable. Loaded only after you accept marketing.

Your choices

On your first visit we ask for consent before optional cookies. You can accept all, keep essential only, or manage categories. You can change your mind anytime.

How long we keep preferences

Your consent choice is stored in your browser (local storage) so we do not re-prompt on every visit. Clearing site data on your device will reset the banner.

International visitors

We serve visitors in many countries. Optional cookies that are not strictly necessary are not set until you give consent. Where laws give you rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data, contact us and we will respond as required.

Third parties

Fonts may load from Google Fonts. Payment, scheduling, and form tools we link to have their own privacy practices—review those when you continue to checkout or booking.

Updates

We may update this page when our tools or legal requirements change. The date at the top shows the latest revision.
