Questions

Asked on Every Fit Call So Far

Is this HIPAA-safe for my practice?
Aitlas is scoped to your marketing and operations: reviews, rankings, GBP, content, competitors, reactivation workflows. It is deliberately kept away from protected patient health information, which keeps your compliance surface clean. We built our company around healthcare marketing, so this line is drawn carefully and on purpose.
How much time does my team actually need to put in?
The Growth Brief takes about four minutes to read. Acting on the priorities is usually 30 to 90 minutes of team time a week, and Aitlas tells you exactly where to spend it. Compare that to the 3 to 5 hours a week of manual auditing it replaces.
We already work with ChiroCandy. Does this stack?
Yes, and it stacks well. Aitlas watches everything, including the work we do together, and routes priorities to the right place: your team, DIY, or our team. If you are an existing client, mention it on your Fit and Activation Call.
What happens after I pay the $500 deposit?
You get a booking link for your Fit and Activation Call within minutes. On that call we confirm fit, map your build, and schedule your discovery session. If either of us decides it is not a fit, the deposit comes back in full. If it is, it applies directly to your $2,500 activation.
What if it does not work for my practice?
The initial term is 90 days, not a year. You will have received roughly 12 Growth Briefs by then, so you will know exactly what Aitlas found, what your team acted on, and what it was worth. If it has not earned its seat, you walk with your knowledge assets.
Why is there an activation fee?
Because there is a real build. Discovery, a private knowledge base, configuration, and QA happen before your first brief. The $2,500 covers a custom build for your specific practice, not a signup form. It is also why we only take ten founding practices at a time.
